Rhino

Status: critically endangered

Species Facts:

  • There are only 5 different species of rhino.
  • Rhinos weigh over 3 tonnes.
  • Both rhino species, black and white rhinos, are in fact grey.
  • A rhino horn is made out of the same stuff as a human fingernail.
  • Rhinos have bad vision.
  • Rhinos communicate through honks, sneezes, and poo.

Endangerment:

  • About 27,000 rhinos remain in the wild.
  • Poaching is the leading cause of the endangerment of rhinos.
  • Rhinos are attacked by humans for their horns
  • Habitat loss and fragmentation are an increasing threat to rhinos.
