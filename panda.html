Panda

Status: critically endangered

Panda Facts:

  • Pandas are solitary creatues
  • Pandas can communicate by chirping, honking, bleating, chomping and barking
  • Pandas are considered a national treasure in China
  • Pandas mainly live in Southeast Asia where they feast on mainly bamboo
  • Pandas have to eat around 26 to 84 pounds of bamboo every day
