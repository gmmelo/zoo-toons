Orangutan

Status: critically endangered

Species Facts:

  • There are 3 different species of orangutans.
  • Orangutans are the largets mammals that live on trees.
  • Orangutans build nests to sleep in
  • Orangutans can live up to 60 years.
  • Orangutans share 96.4% of our genes.

Endangerment Facts:

  • There are between 50,000-65,000 orangutans left in the world.
  • An average of 2,000 to 3,000 orangutans are killed every year.
  • It is estimated that they will be extinct in 50 years.
  • Loss of habitat through deforestationand palm oil plantations.
  • Illegal hunting and illegal pet trade also affect their population.
